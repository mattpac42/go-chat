## Core Frameworks

### The Core Four Methods

The complete universe of lead generation possibilities:

1. **Warm Outreach**: One-to-one private contact with people who know you (warm audience)
2. **Post Free Content**: One-to-many public content building warm audience at scale
3. **Cold Outreach**: One-to-one private contact with strangers (cold audience)
4. **Run Paid Ads**: One-to-many public advertising to strangers at scale

**Critical Principle**: These four methods exhaust all advertising possibilities. Mastery = all four + sufficient skill + sufficient volume.

### The Three Constraints

Every lead generation problem stems from one of three constraints:

1. **Awareness**: Do people know you exist? (Solution: Content + Paid Ads)
2. **Interest**: Are they showing engagement? (Solution: Better lead magnets + offer positioning)
3. **Action**: Are they converting to customers? (Solution: Better CTAs + sales process)

### The Four Lead Getters (Leverage)

Exponential growth requires others generating leads for you:

1. **Customers** (Referrals): Satisfied customers bringing new prospects
2. **Employees** (Team): Hired staff executing Core Four at scale
3. **Agencies** (Partnerships): Specialists bringing skills you lack
4. **Affiliates** (Networks): Partners promoting your offers to their audiences

**Goal**: Scenario 4—lead getters who get lead getters (true exponential growth)

### Hook-Retain-Reward (Content Unit)

The smallest unit of valuable content:

1. **Hook**: Get attention (Topics + Headlines + Format)
   - Topics: Far past, recent past, present, trending, manufactured
   - Headlines: Recency, relevancy, celebrity, proximity, conflict, unusual, ongoing
   - Format: Match platform expectations

2. **Retain**: Keep attention (Lists, Steps, Stories)
   - Lists: Numbered items following theme
   - Steps: Sequential actions accomplishing goal
   - Stories: Events with lessons/takeaways

3. **Reward**: Satisfy curiosity (Value per second)
   - Clearly deliver on hook promise
   - No such thing as too long, only too boring
   - Audience growth = successful reward

### The ACA Framework (Conversations)

Acknowledge-Compliment-Ask structure for all warm/cold outreach:

- **A**cknowledge: Restate what they said (active listening)
- **C**ompliment: Tie to positive character trait
- **A**sk: Lead conversation toward your offer

### The 7-Level Advertising Roadmap

1. **Level 1**: Friends know about you (warm outreach only)
2. **Level 2**: Consistent personal capacity (warm + content)
3. **Level 3**: Employee help (first hires scaling Core Four)
4. **Level 4**: Product-market fit (25%+ referrals, consistent word of mouth)
5. **Level 5**: Multichannel scale (2+ Core Four methods, multiple platforms)
6. **Level 6**: Executive leadership (veteran department heads running channels)
7. **Level 7**: $100M+ machine (full leverage operating without you)
